DELICIOUS POTATO BHAAJI

To make this savory potato bhaaji you will need::

  • Potatoes boiled and chopped
  • Indian spices
  • Chopped onions

How to make:

  1. In oil add spices and onion
  2. Stir well
  3. Add potato and stir well
  4. Serve hot

Enjoy this hot and tasty potato bhaaji with roti!
