Tasty Japanese Bear Curry

To make this you will need

  • Japanese curry chunks
  • Broccoli
  • Chicken
  • Japanese seaweed nori
  • sliced cheese
  • Japanese puchi tomato
  • Cooked rice

How to make:

  1. Saute chicken chunks in oil
  2. Add Curry chunk in hot water and stir
  3. Add the chicken and Broccoli and stir
  4. Place rice on plate in the shape of bear
  5. Cut nori and place on rice
  6. Cut heart and other shapes out of cheese and arrange on bear
  7. Slice puchi tomato in half and place on bear

Serve hot!
