SWEET CARROT HALWA

To make this sweet carrot halwa you will need::

  • Carrots
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

How to make:

  1. Finely grate carrots
  2. In ghee add the grated carrots and stir continuously
  3. After the color of the carrots changes, add milk and sugar
  4. Stir for 15 minutes
  5. Refrigerate for a couple of hours

Have fun eating this tasty dessert! ^^
