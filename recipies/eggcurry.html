TASTY EGG CURRY

To make this tasty curry you will need::

  • 2 Boiled eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Chopped Onions
  • Indian spices

How to make:

  1. In oil add indian spices
  2. Stir and add chopped onion
  3. Add tomatoes and stir well for a few minutes
  4. Add some more indian spices and boiled eggs
  5. Serve hot

Enjoy the tasty curry ^^
